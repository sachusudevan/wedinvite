Our Story

Chapter One

First Meeting

Among countless souls, fate chose two strangers to meet. One conversation became a melody, and two hearts quietly began writing their forever.

Chapter Two

The First Date

A single introduction became a thousand cherished memories. From our first conversation to our first selfie, our first lunch, our first journey together—every "first" gently wove two hearts into one beautiful forever.

Two souls, one heartbeat — every love story is beautiful, but ours is my favourite.

Chapter Three

Falling In Love

Love arrived gently, without announcing itself. A single day became countless conversations, every call drew our hearts a little closer, and every shared thought became another thread in the tapestry of our forever. Somewhere between the good mornings and the good nights, you became my greatest blessing and my sweetest tomorrow.

Chapter Four

The Proposal

Every rose carried a silent "I love you," and every little surprise held a piece of your heart. In that beautiful moment, I realized that love isn't measured by grand gestures, but by the countless little ways we make each other feel cherished.
